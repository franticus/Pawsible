Privacy Policy — Node

At Node, we respect your privacy and are committed to handling your personal information responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, how long we keep it, and the rights you have regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the data necessary to operate our services and improve your experience:

  • Information you provide: such as your name, email address, or details you submit via forms.
  • Technical data: including IP address, browser type, operating system, and device settings.
  • Usage data: interactions with our pages, time spent, navigation patterns, and clicks.
  • Cookies and local storage: to support login (if applicable), save preferences, and enable analytics.

2. Why We Collect This Data

  • To provide and maintain the services you request.
  • To respond to inquiries or support requests you send us.
  • To analyze usage and improve performance, design, and content.
  • To comply with legal obligations or regulatory requirements.

3. Cookies

We use functional cookies that are essential for the site to work, and analytical cookies (only with your consent where required) to understand how our services are used. You can manage or disable cookies at any time in your browser settings.

4. Data Retention

We keep personal data only as long as it is necessary for the purposes described: contact form data up to 24 months, technical logs up to 12 months, and analytics data according to the retention periods of the tools we use or until you withdraw your consent.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or misuse your information. We may share limited data with trusted service providers (hosting, analytics, email delivery) strictly for the purpose of operating and improving our services. Data may also be disclosed if legally required by competent authorities.

6. International Transfers

If data is transferred outside your country, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) under the GDPR or equivalent mechanisms, where required by law.

7. Your Rights

You remain in control of your data. You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information. You may also object to or restrict certain processing, and withdraw consent for analytics or communications at any time.

8. Updates

From time to time, we may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in law, technology, or our practices. Any significant updates will be clearly communicated on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at games@node.com. We will respond promptly and transparently to ensure your trust is maintained.

Privacy Policy — Pawsible

At Pawsible, we respect your privacy and handle personal information responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use and share it, how long we keep it, and the choices and rights you have. This Policy applies to our website, app, and related services (collectively, the “Services”).

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve the Services:

  • Account & contact data: name, email, phone, address, pet details you provide.
  • Service data: booking information, preferences, notes about pet routines or care needs.
  • Usage & device data: IP address, browser/OS, device identifiers, pages viewed, clicks, session duration.
  • Location data (when enabled): GPS information to show walk routes, visit start/end, or nearby carers.
  • Media you submit: photos, messages, reviews.
  • Cookies & local storage: to keep you signed in, remember preferences, and support analytics (with consent where required).

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, operate, and secure the Services (create bookings, match with carers, send updates).
  • To show real-time updates (e.g., GPS-tracked walk maps, photos, and visit reports).
  • To communicate with you (confirmations, support, important notices).
  • To improve performance, design, and content via analytics and research.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

3. Legal Bases (GDPR)

Where the GDPR applies, we process personal data based on: (i) contract performance (providing the Services), (ii) legitimate interests (service improvement, security, fraud prevention), (iii) consent (non-essential cookies, marketing, precise location when required), and (iv) legal obligations.

4. Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use essential cookies for core functionality (e.g., login, security) and analytics cookies to understand usage. Where required by law, we ask for your consent before setting non-essential cookies. You can manage cookies in your browser settings; disabling some cookies may affect site functionality.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with trusted service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, email/SMS delivery, payment processing) strictly to operate and improve the Services, under confidentiality and data-protection commitments. We may disclose data if required by law or to protect rights, safety, or prevent fraud/abuse.

6. International Transfers

If data is transferred to countries with different data-protection standards, we implement appropriate safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) where applicable.

7. Data Retention

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purposes described above: account and booking records for the life of your account (and as required by law thereafter), support communications up to 24 months, technical logs up to 12 months, and analytics data according to the retention policies of the tools we use or until you withdraw consent.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data; object to or restrict certain processing; and withdraw consent at any time (e.g., analytics or precise location). To exercise rights, contact us at hello@pawsible.com. You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

9. California Privacy (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents can request access to and deletion of personal information and opt out of certain data sharing. We do not sell personal information. To make a request, email hello@pawsible.com.

10. Children’s Privacy

The Services are not directed to children under 13 (or as defined by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us to delete it.

11. Security

We implement administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect personal information. No system is 100% secure, but we continually work to enhance our safeguards.

12. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Policy to reflect changes in law or our practices. We will post the updated version with a new “Last updated” date and, where appropriate, notify you through the Services.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions or requests about this Policy or your data, contact us at hello@pawsible.com. Address: 1234 Innovation Boulevard, Suite 567, Austin, TX 78701, United States.

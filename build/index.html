Why Pet Parents
Choose Us

We know entrusting someone with your pet is a big decision. That’s why we go above and beyond to deliver not just a service, but an experience you can feel completely confident in.

Our entire service is built around three core promises: safety you can trust, transparency you can see, and care that feels like family. While other services offer pet care, we provide peace of mind through our meticulous approach to every detail of our service. These fundamental principles are what make us the trusted choice for thousands of discerning pet parents across the country.
Vetted & Insured
Every carer passes a comprehensive background check and is fully insured for your peace of mind. We verify experience and require pet first aid certification so you can relax knowing your companion is in capable hands.
Real-Time Updates
Never miss a moment of your pet's day. Get GPS-tracked walk maps, adorable photo updates, and detailed reports sent directly to your phone. It's the next best thing to being there yourself.
Tailored to Your Pet
We understand every pet is unique. Our smart matching connects you with carers who specialize in your pet's breed, energy level, and specific needs, ensuring they receive the perfect level of care and attention.
